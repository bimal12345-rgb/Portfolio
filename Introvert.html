I am not him.

Posted on

Most people would never guess that he is an introvert guy who was very shy and never really talked to anyone. He always kept to himself and never really made any friends. He was content with just being by himself. One day, he decided to go out and talk to people. He started by just saying hello to people and then he began to talk to them more. He found that he really enjoyed talking to people and he started to make friends.

IHe's always the life of the party, always cracking jokes and always eager to get out and about. But the truth is, he gets exhausted easily from being around people too much. He prefers quieter, more intimate gatherings where he can really get to know people. That's not to say he doesn't enjoy a good party - he just doesn't like being the center of attention. He is a great listener and people often find themselves confiding in him. He's always happy to lend a listening ear and offer sage advice.
